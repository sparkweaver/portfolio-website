My Journey

My programming career began after I realized that I wanted to work in a field I was passionate about, something that would make me excited to go to work every day. This decision led me to pursue education in technology. I found the perfect blend of hardware and software in the "Master of Science in Intelligent Systems" program at Halmstad University. After two years, I discovered that my true passion lay in software development, and I intended to switch to a computer science degree.

However, when COVID-19 hit, I decided to stay an additional year in my current program, treating it as a siesta year and focusing on courses that piqued my interest. The following year, I enrolled in the "Software Engineering" program at Linnéuniversitet. After three years, I graduated with a Bachelor's in Computer Science. Now, I’m actively seeking a job in the field while continuing to work on hobby projects and pursuing online certifications.
