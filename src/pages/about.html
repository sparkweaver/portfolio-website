My Journey

My programming career began after I realized that I wanted to work in a field I was passionate about, something that would make me excited to go to work every day. This decision led me to pursue an education in technology. I initially found the perfect blend of hardware and software in the Master of Science in Intelligent Systems program at Halmstad University to see which side of the coin I preferred. After two years, I discovered that my interests lay in software development, and I intended to switch to a computer science degree. However, when COVID-19 hit, I decided to stay an additional year, treating it as a siesta year and focusing on courses that piqued my interest.

The following year, I enrolled in the Software Engineering program at Linnéuniversitet. After three years, I graduated with a Bachelor's in Computer Science. Now, I’m actively seeking a job in the field while continuing to work on hobby projects and pursuing online certifications.
